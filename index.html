Privacy Policy for Recipe Vault

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Recipe Vault respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how Recipe Vault handles user data and your privacy while using the application.

Information We Collect

Recipe Vault does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The application:

  • Does not require account registration
  • Does not collect names or email addresses
  • Does not collect phone numbers
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not collect contact information
  • Does not collect photos, videos, or media files
  • Does not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes

Offline Usage

Recipe Vault operates entirely offline.

All recipes, favorites, meal plans, notes, and other user-created content are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to external servers or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

Recipe Vault does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • User tracking technologies
  • Third-party data collection services
  • Cloud storage services

Data Sharing

Because Recipe Vault does not collect personal information, no user data is sold, shared, rented, transferred, or disclosed to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Recipe Vault does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users. The application is designed to function without collecting personal data.

Data Security

All information created within the app remains on your device. Since no personal information is collected or transmitted, users maintain control over their locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in future versions of the application and, where applicable, updated in the Google Play Store listing or official website.
